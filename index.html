Lion
King of the Jungle
Description:

Lions, the iconic big cats of the African savannah, are known for their majestic appearance and social behaviors. Recognized as the "King of the Jungle," lions are powerful predators with a unique social structure.

Key Features:
  • Mane: Majestic manes, typically darker in color in males.
  • Habitat: Grasslands, savannahs, and open woodlands in Africa.
  • Social Structure: Live in prides with a hierarchical structure.
  • Roar: Distinctive roar audible from miles away.
Behavior:

Lions are social animals, living in prides led by a dominant male. They work together in hunting, with lionesses doing most of the hunting.

Diet:

Carnivores with a varied diet including wildebeest, zebras, and other large ungulates. Lions are apex predators in their ecosystems.
